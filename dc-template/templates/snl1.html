4. Integrated Systems Testing (IST)

The "Level 5" Commissioning Protocol.

L5: The "Black Building" Test

Objective: Validate system resilience during a complete utility failure at full thermal load.

Step 1: Utility Cut

Simulate loss of Grid. Verify Generators start & accept load in < 15 seconds.

Step 2: Transient Ride-through

Verify UPS holds IT load with 0% fluctuation during the transfer.

Step 3: Thermal Stability

Verify Chillers restart (Fast Start). Hall temp must remain within ASHRAE A2 limits.
